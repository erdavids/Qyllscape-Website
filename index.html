Qyllscape

Why not write a book?

Qyllscape is a writing tool that is designed to fit into your environment, letting you responsively customize whatever is helpful, from layout to lighting to colors.

Platforms

Initial release will be on Steam, which will give users access to cloud-saving between devices, a Qyllscape community hub, and Steam achievements related to writing progress.

A stand-alone version will be available for users that do not wish to use Steam, but it will be released later, and will be a simplified version of Qyllscape.
