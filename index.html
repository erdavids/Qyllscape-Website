Your Environment Matters

Qyllscape started because I felt like most editors are so harsh to use, and never fit into the themes of my own writing environment. Over time Qyllscape developed in a suite of options that let you responsively customize whatever is helpful, from layout and colors, to the ambient candle-lighting that plays out on your editor.

The Love Of Writing

Qyllscape is a nearly minimalist tool that is not intended to replace all other editors. Instead, it is intended to make it easier to sit down and write during the times that it feels most difficult.

Development

There are many ways in which Qyllscape can improve, and updates to core functionality such as editing, exporting/importing, and all convencience tools will be released as often as possible. All users will receive updates for free, and the community hub allows for user feedback which is always considered a priority.

Platforms

Initial release is available on Steam for both Windows and MacOS, which will give users access to cloud-saving between devices, a Qyllscape community hub, and Steam achievements related to writing progress.

A stand-alone version will eventually be available for users that do not wish to use Steam, but it will be released later, and will be a simplified version of Qyllscape.
